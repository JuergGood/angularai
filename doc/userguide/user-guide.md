## Getting Started
To access the application, navigate to the frontend URL (typically `http://localhost`). You will be prompted to log in. If you don't have an account, you can register a new one.

### Login
Enter your username and password to access your account.

### Registration
If you are a new user, click on the "Register" link. Provide your first name, last name, desired login, email address, and password.

## Dashboard
The Dashboard provides an overview of your activities and the system status:
- **Summary Cards**: Quick stats on Open Tasks, Active Users, Completed Tasks, and Today's Logs.
- **Task Overview**: A visual distribution of tasks by status (Open, In Progress, Completed).
- **Recent Activity**: A list of the latest actions performed in the system.
- **Priority Tasks**: A list of high-priority tasks that need your attention.

## Task Management
The Task Management page allows you to organize your work:
- **Add Task**: Click the "Add Task" button to create a new task. You can specify a title, description, due date, priority, and status.
- **Filter and Sort**: Filter tasks by status (Open, In Progress, Completed) or reset sorting to view them by priority.
- **Edit Task**: Click the edit icon on a task card to modify its details.
- **Delete Task**: Click the delete icon to remove a task.
- **Drag and Drop**: You can reorder tasks by dragging them using the handle (available when not filtering).

## User Profile
In the Profile section, you can view your personal details, including your name, email, and assigned role.

## Logout
To securely leave the application, click the "Logout" button in the side navigation menu.
